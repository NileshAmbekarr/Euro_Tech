Our Founder

Mr. Aniket Suryawanshi

Mr. Aniket Suryawanshi is the visionary founder of Eurocore Technologies, dedicated to empowering students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed in the modern technology industry. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Engineering and has further strengthened his expertise through Full Stack Development with specialization in Flutter.

With a strong passion for teaching and mentoring, he actively provides professional training in modern technologies, career guidance, and placement support to help students build successful careers in the IT industry. Along with technical training, he also guides students preparing for competitive examinations, helping them develop the right strategy, discipline, and problem-solving approach required for success.

Through Eurocore Technologies, his vision is to create a platform where students can gain industry-ready skills, practical experience through real-world projects, and strong career guidance. His mission is to bridge the gap between academic education and industry expectations, enabling students to become confident developers and future technology leaders.

Who We Are

Eurocore Technologies is a career-focused technology training company committed to building the next generation of skilled developers and AI professionals.

Our experienced mentors have trained hundreds of students who are now working in top technology companies and fast-growing startups.

Our industry-aligned curriculum includes Full Stack Development, MERN Stack, and emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning fundamentals.

Through live coding sessions, real-world projects, and hands-on problem solving, we ensure every student develops practical, job-ready skills.

Mission

To empower individuals with practical, job-ready skills in Full Stack Development and Artificial Intelligence.

Vision

To become a trusted global platform for high-quality tech education and build a community of future-ready developers.

Our Values

Excellence in education, integrity in practice, innovation in teaching, and commitment to student success.
